Mr.Milind Shirude

FOUNDER

With decades of experience in the construction industry, Mr. Milind Shirude is the guiding force behind Atharva Enterprises. His vision, discipline, and unwavering commitment to quality have shaped the company's growth and reputation over the years.

Under his leadership, the company has consistently delivered projects that reflect integrity, reliability, and long-term value - earning the trust of customers across generations.

Mr.Atharva Shirude

DIRECTOR

With a background in Civil Engineering (MIT Pune) and an M.S. in Construction Management (NC State University), Atharva Shirude bridges the gap between technical infrastructure and premium lifestyle experiences.

Currently, Atharva leads the Pune Real Estate Division while serving as Director of Enrise by Sayaji, Nashik. Having honed his skills internationally at the North Carolina Department of Transportation and domestically at L&T ECC, he applies a disciplined, data-driven approach to real estate and hospitality. His vision is defined by a commitment to global professional standards, delivering excellence across the urban landscape of Pune and the hospitality sector of Nashik.

Over the decades, Atharva Enterprises journey has evolved into more than just building structures; it has been about understanding how people live and what truly makes a space feel complete. Every project is designed with a singular intent: making life simpler, more comfortable, and more meaningful.
